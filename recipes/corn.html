Corn rings

Description

Wether you use it as a typical entry dish or even as a snack, This corn rings are going to become not only one of your favorite ways to cook a corn, but aslo your life saver for when your don't want to spend so much time in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion pow
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 2 ears fresh corn, sliced into 6 equal-sized rings
  • sea salt to taste
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Combine butter, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, and Cajun seasonning in a microwave-safe dish and cook in the micowave until butter is fully melted about 1 minute. Remove from microwave and stir.
  3. Use tongs to dip a corn rings into the spiced butter, making sure to coat it thoroughly in the spices. Transfer to a plate and continue until all of the rings have been dipped. There will be butter remaining.
  4. Heat an oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Once heated, place corn rings into the skillet, season them individually with sea salt, and let them sizzle away until a nice sear has developed about 3 minutes. Flip and use a basting brush to brush remaining butter onto the seared sides of the rings. Season again with salt and continue to cook for 2 more minutes.
  5. Transger the skillet into the preheated oven and cok for 10 minutes. carefully flip the rings and cool for 5 more minutes.
  6. Remove from the ove and trasfer to a serving platter. Sprinkle chives over top and serve with ranch dressing along with corn skewers for picking up individual rings.
