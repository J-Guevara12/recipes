Chicken Schnitzel

Description

Wether you use it as a typical entry dish or even as a snack, This corn rings are going to become not only one of your favorite ways to cook a corn, but aslo your life saver for when your don't want to spend so much time in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoons crushed garlic
  • 3 cups bread crumbs, or as needed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon grounf black pepper
  • vegetable oil fro frying
  • 12 boneless chicken breast halves, pounded very thin
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, honey, Dijon mustard, soy sauce, and garlick together i a bowl. Mix bread crumbs salt and pepper together in a shallow bowl
  2. Heat 1/2 to 1 inch of vegetable oil in a heavy skillet to 350°F(175°C). You can also use a deep fryer
  3. Put chicken slices in the egg misture and knead until fully coated. Press chicken in the bread crumbs until evenly coated
  4. Working in Batches, carefuly place chicken in the hot oil using tongs no longer pink in the center and browned on the outside,3 to 4 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels and serve with lemon wedges.
